The International Airlines Technical Pool (IATP) was founded in 1948, initially in Europe. It has evolved into a global pool organization with members spanning all continents. IATP is recognized worldwide within the aviation industry as a not-for-profit "Service Cooperative."

Over its 76 year history, IATP has expanded its membership base from 10 airlines in 1948 to more than 130 airlines today, supported by over 40 OEM/Service providers and around 45 "Sponsored" airlines. This growth reflects the increasing value airlines place on technical resource sharing.

Core Purpose

IATP serves as a platform for collaborative resource sharing among airlines, enhancing operational efficiency, reducing costs, and improving safety across the global aviation network.

The organization operates through a cooperative model where member airlines pool technical resources, share expertise, and provide mutual support. This collaboration extends across 890 global stations, ensuring comprehensive coverage and 24/7/365 support capabilities.

IATP (International Airlines Technical Pool) is a global not-for-profit organization that facilitates technical resource sharing among airlines. Founded in 1948, we provide parts pooling, line maintenance, aircraft recovery, and equipment pooling services to over 130 member airlines worldwide.

Airlines interested in joining IATP can contact our membership team through the Member Portal or via our contact page. Our team will guide you through the membership process, including requirements, benefits, and integration procedures.

IATP provides four main pillars of service:

  • Parts Pooling: Access to 4,000+ spare parts across 890 global stations
  • Line Maintenance: 24/7 technical assistance at over 890 locations worldwide
  • Aircraft Recovery: Swift recovery services to minimize operational disruptions
  • Equipment Pooling: Access to specialized ground support equipment globally

IATP conferences are held bi-annually in different locations around the world, hosted by member airlines. These gatherings provide opportunities for networking, knowledge sharing, and discussing industry developments. Check our Events page for upcoming conference details.

IATP operates with strict adherence to local and international competition and anti-trust laws. All members and representatives are bound by these legal requirements, and the organization maintains transparent operations that comply with all applicable regulations across jurisdictions.
