Association Guidelines

1
General
Many of the people who have enjoyed the association and friendship with colleagues through the IATP have wanted to have a way of continuing these contacts even after their active participation in the IATP business ceases. For this purpose, the IATP PIONAIRS Association was founded in 1984.
2
Name
The PIONAIRS Association shall be entitled: "The PIONAIRS Association of the International Airlines Technical Pool (IATP-PA)."
3
Purpose
The purpose of the PIONAIRS Association shall be to foster and preserve international friendships developed among persons who have served as airline delegates to the IATP.
