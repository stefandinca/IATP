HERO TITLE: How to become an Associate Member

Heading: A supplier is only eligible to become a party to the Associate Membership Agreement if, at the discretion of the Advisory Committee / Management Board, it:
- is a supplier of goods or services to the aviation industry and particularly to the IATP membership in any of the following areas:
    Spares pooling (Supplier provided parts)
    Component, engine or airframe MRO
    Engines Provider
    Line Maintenance
    Training
    Logistics
    Transportation services.
- is not a holder of an Air Carrier Operations Certificate and/or not operating as an airline.
- holds the appropriate government certifications and licenses for the goods or services supplied.
- at stations within its own country of registration the pooling of technical facilities or services under the Associate Membership Agreement is not in any way restricted or prohibited.
- has attended five (5) IATP Conferences out of the last consecutive seven (7) prior to application submission (not required for the Exempt Associate Members)
- has attended the Supplier training session on Associate Membership and the Introduction to IATP session during the Conference prior to application submission.
- has provided a letter of recommendation from an IATP Member Airline on that airlineâ€™s letterhead.
- has been recommended for membership by the Supplier Services Committee based upon the:
- value the applicant brings to IATP Member Airlines, and
- requirements of those same Members.
- has paid the applicable entry fee.
